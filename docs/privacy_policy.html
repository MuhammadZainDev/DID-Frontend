DuaonAI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Welcome to DuaonAI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

DuaonAI collects minimal personal information, limited to:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and name.
  • User Preferences: Settings such as language preferences, theme settings, and favorites.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To personalize your experience
  • To improve our application
  • To communicate with you about account-related matters
  • To save your favorite duas

3. Data Storage

Your information is stored in the following ways:

  • Local Storage: Some data like preferences and cached duas are stored locally on your device for offline access.
  • Server Storage: Account information and favorites are stored on our secure servers.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted service providers who help us operate our application.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Third-Party Services

DuaonAI may use third-party services that collect information. These services include:

  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our application
  • Hosting Services: To store data and provide our services

These third-party services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to request correction of your data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at the email address provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

DuaonAI does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Permissions Required

DuaonAI may require the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save duas and media files for offline access
  • Internet: To download duas, audio files, and update content
  • Notification: For prayer time reminders (if enabled)
  • Location: Optional for calculating accurate prayer times based on your location
